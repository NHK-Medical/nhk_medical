What is Sleep Apnea?

Sleep apnea is a sleep disorder characterized by breathing pauses during sleep due to relaxed throat muscles. These pauses can disrupt sleep patterns and lead to various symptoms, like fragmented sleep, loud snoring, and daytime fatigue.

Why do you need a Sleep Study Test?  

  • Excessive Daytime Sleepiness
  • Difficulty Falling or Staying Asleep
  • Experiencing loud snoring
  • Irregular Work Schedules
  • Fatigue and Lack of Energy
  • Difficulty in Concentrating
  • Pauses in Breathing During Sleep
